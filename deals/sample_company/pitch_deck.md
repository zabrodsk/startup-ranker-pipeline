# NovaBite - AI-Powered Food Waste Reduction

## Slide 1: Cover
NovaBite Inc.
"Turning food waste into smart savings"
Series A | Q1 2026

## Slide 2: Problem
- Restaurants waste 30-40% of purchased food, costing the average restaurant $25,000/year.
- Global food waste is estimated at 1.3 billion tons per year (FAO).
- Existing inventory management tools lack predictive capabilities.

## Slide 3: Solution
NovaBite uses AI demand forecasting to help restaurants:
- Predict daily ingredient needs with 92% accuracy.
- Auto-generate purchase orders optimized for shelf life.
- Reduce food waste by 35-50% within 3 months of deployment.

## Slide 4: Product
- Cloud-based SaaS dashboard integrated with POS systems.
- Machine learning models trained on 2+ years of transaction data per customer.
- Mobile app for kitchen staff with real-time alerts.
- API integrations: Toast, Square, Clover, Lightspeed.
