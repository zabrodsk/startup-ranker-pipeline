Features

Everything your team
needs to move faster.

Built for investment teams dealing with high volume, complex documentation, and the need for consistent, defensible evaluation criteria across every deal.

Evidence-Grounded QA

Every answer traces back to a specific passage in the source document. Citations are embedded in every output — no hallucinations, no black-box reasoning. Every claim is verifiable.

Source Citation
"…the company achieved €2.4M ARR with 142% net revenue retention, as confirmed across slides 8–11 and the financial appendix provided."
Confidence: 0.94 Slide 9 · Financial Appendix p.3
Pro Argument
"Strong network effects with 3.4× viral coefficient supported by organic growth data. Founder network validates distribution moat."
Confidence: 0.88 Market Analysis · Slide 14
Multi-Format Ingestion
PDF, PPTX, DOCX, XLSX, CSV. Any format your founders send. Hierarchical text chunking preserves document structure for accurate evidence retrieval.
Pro/Contra Arguments
Structured investment thesis with devil's advocate critiques built in. Each argument scored by confidence and relevance, with evidence citations attached.
Composite Scoring
Three dimensions scored automatically: Strategy Fit, Team Quality, Upside Potential. Consistent, comparable, and defensible across every deal in your pipeline.
Company Chat
Ask follow-up questions on any company. Answers grounded in saved evidence, with full citation history shared across your entire investment team in real time.
Excel Export
Board-ready reports with rankings, executive summaries, and a complete citation audit trail. One click, every deal, ready to share with partners and LPs.
